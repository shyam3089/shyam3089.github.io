TV Remote

Privacy policy for TV Remote app users.

Microphone Use for Voice Commands

TV Remote may request microphone access so you can use voice commands to control your TV. Microphone access is used only when you choose to start a voice command feature.

Voice input is used to recognize your command and convert it into a TV remote action, such as searching, opening an app, changing volume, or navigating supported TV features. We do not use microphone access to listen in the background.

You can deny or disable microphone permission at any time in your device settings. If you disable microphone access, manual remote controls will still work, but voice command features may not be available.

Information We Collect

TV Remote may collect basic app usage information, device information, diagnostics, and crash reports to help us improve stability, performance, and user experience.

We do not ask for sensitive personal information unless it is required for a specific TV Remote feature. When TV Remote requires permission, the permission is used only for the feature described in the app or this policy.

App Permissions

Depending on the TV Remote features you use, permissions may include:

  • Microphone: Used by TV Remote for voice command input when you activate the feature.
  • Network access: Used to connect to supported TVs, online services, updates, ads, analytics, or support features.
  • Device information: Used for app compatibility, diagnostics, crash reporting, and performance improvement.

How We Use Information

  • To provide TV Remote features and maintain app functionality.
  • To improve TV Remote reliability, performance, and usability.
  • To detect, prevent, and resolve technical issues.
  • To comply with applicable legal and platform requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted third-party services for app analytics, crash reporting, advertising, hosting, or platform services used by TV Remote. These providers are expected to handle data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through one of our apps, contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

Security and Retention

We use reasonable measures to protect information handled through TV Remote. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain information only for as long as needed to provide app features, improve our apps, comply with legal obligations, or resolve issues.

LiteList

Privacy policy for LiteList app users.

LiteList helps users create, organize, and manage lists. The app does not require microphone or camera permissions.

Information We Collect

LiteList may store list names, list items, categories, saved items, and app preferences that you create while using the app. This information is used to provide list management features.

LiteList may collect basic app usage information, device information, diagnostics, and crash reports to help us improve stability, performance, and user experience.

App Permissions

LiteList does not need microphone or camera permissions.

  • Microphone: Not required or used by LiteList.
  • Camera: Not required or used by LiteList.
  • Network access: May be used for updates, ads, analytics, crash reporting, or support features.
  • Device information: Used for app compatibility, diagnostics, crash reporting, and performance improvement.

How We Use Information

  • To provide LiteList features and maintain app functionality.
  • To save and display your lists, categories, saved items, and preferences.
  • To improve LiteList reliability, performance, and usability.
  • To detect, prevent, and resolve technical issues.
  • To comply with applicable legal and platform requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted third-party services for app analytics, crash reporting, advertising, hosting, or platform services used by LiteList. These providers are expected to handle data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Security and Retention

We use reasonable measures to protect information handled through LiteList. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain information only for as long as needed to provide app features, improve our apps, comply with legal obligations, or resolve issues.
